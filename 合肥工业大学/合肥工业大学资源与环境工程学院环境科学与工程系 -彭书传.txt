The research results show that the removal rate of COD is from 70% to 86% and that of NH__(3)-N from 53% to 79% under the organic loading of 4~12 kgCOD/(m~3·d) and the hydraulic loading of (1~4 m~3/(m~2·h)) with the air-water ratio being 1∶1~5∶1. While the hydraulic loading is low, simultaneous nitrification and denitrification can be realized by controlling the air-water ratio in UBAF.
