王汝渠，浙江大学教育部长江讲座教授。毕业于美国波士顿大学经济系，现为加拿大皇后大学经济系爵士终身教授。他长期致力于拍卖理论、方面的研究，并运用博弈论方法对各种拍卖机制进行深入的分析，在政府和公司的采购行为中如何通过设计更好的机制以获取效率和收益等方面作出了重要的贡献，他的研究成果曾发表在《美国经济评论》、《博弈论与经济行为》、《经济理论杂志》等世界顶级经济学杂志，在国际学术界引起广泛关注，是一位在国际经济学界有重要影响的华人经济学家。EXPERIENCE2004-2005 Visiting ProfessorEconomics Department, National Univresity of Singapore, Singapore2003-2004 ProfessorEconomics Department, University of Colorado at Boulder, Boulder, Colorado, U.S.A.2001-present Sir John A MacDonald ProfessorEconomics Department, Queen's University, Kingston, Ontario, Canada1996-2001 Associate ProfessorEconomics Department, Queen's University, Kingston, Ontario, Canada1996-1997 Visiting Assistant ProfessorMEDS, Kellogg Graduate School of Management, Northwestern University Evanston, Illinois, U.S.A.1990-1996 Assistant ProfessorEconomics Department, Queen's University, Kingston, Ontario, CanadaEDUCATIONM.A., Ph.D.Economics Department, Boston University, Boston, Massachusetts, U.S.A.B.S.Mathematics Department, Fudan University, Shanghai, ChinaPh.D DISSERTATION1991 "Common-Value Auctions: Theory and Applications", Advisors: Robert Rosenthal and Andrew Weiss, Boston UniversityAWARDS2002-2003 Department of Economics Award in Research Excellence, Queen's UniversityBOARD OF EDITORS2000-presentAnnals of Economics and Finance2006-presentThe B.E.
