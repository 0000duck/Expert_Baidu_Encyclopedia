。主讲上海市精品课程“高分子化学”（本科）、“高分子化学实验”（本科）（上海市精品课程教学团队）和“高分子化学”（公选课）等课程。现为《高分子化学》课程主讲教师。承担了学校各类课程建设和教改项目四项，发表教学研究论文6篇。参编教材1本。2007年获理工教学奖，2008年获校优秀教学成果一等奖。作为项目负责人和主要承担人，承担和完成了上海市科委，国家863项目、国防科工委、国际国内合作等多项科研项目。在包括ChemistryofMaterials,Macromolecules，JournalofMaterialsChemistry,Nanoscale和Carbon等高分子材料领域的专业学术刊物上共发表SCI论文50余篇。申请发明专利16项，其中已授权10项，公开专利4项。1.Yi Chen,Shuo Zhang,,et al.Preparation of Solution-Processable Reduced Graphene Oxide/ Polybenzoxazole Nanocomposites with Improved Dielectric Properties[J].Macromolecules, 2015, 48, 365- 372.2.Yi Chen,Xiaoyun Liu,,et al.γ-Fe2O3–MWNT/poly(p-phenylenebenzobisoxazole) composites with excellent microwave absorption performance and thermal stability[J].Nanoscale,2014, 6, 6440- 6447.3.Yi Chen, ,et al.Preparation of thermostable PBO/graphene nanocomposites with high dielectric constant[J].Nanotechnology,2013,24,245702.4.,Xilong Liu,Qing Wang,et al.Synthesis of acid-soluble graphene and its use in producing a reduced graphene oxide–poly(benzobisoxazole) composite[J].J.
