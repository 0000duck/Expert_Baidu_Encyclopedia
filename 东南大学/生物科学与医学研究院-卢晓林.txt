We study surfaces/interfaces of biological systems/polymer materials using advanced nonlinear optical spectroscopy. We also expand the application of nonlinear optical spectroscopy to complex surfaces/interfaces, for example materials in nano- or micron-scale. What we do aims at understanding and applying materials in different length scales, to satisfy our own curiosity and for the benifit of our society.
