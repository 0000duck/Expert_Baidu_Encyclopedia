回春萍 女、教师。2002年获南开大学学位。英美文学回春萍，外语学院助教，专业英美文学，主要从事当代美国文学研究。 目前负责本科一年级英语精读及语音教学。此外，当前主要进行当代女性文学的研究，如．沃克，托尼．等。2002年，在硕士毕业论文中，她根据沃克的《紫色》，《拥有快乐之谜》和《我宠物的殿堂》三部小说完成了《追寻幻想中的现实》。Hui Chunping, Teaching Assistant, majors in literature, particularly Contemporary American Literature. She is currently teaching freshmen intensive English reading and pronunciation. Apart from teaching, she studies contemporary American women writers, such as Alice Walker, Toni Morrison. In 2002, she completed her graduate thesis titled Fantasy － Seeking for Alternative Reality, which is based on Alice Walker’s three novels Color Purple, Possessing the Secret of Joy, and The Temple of My Familiar.
