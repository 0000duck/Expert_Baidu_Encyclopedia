张庆柱，男，汉族，1956年生，山东济宁市人，中国医学科学院&#8226;中国协和医科大学神经药理学博士研究生毕业，赴日本新潟药学和应用生命科学大学（Niigata University of Pharmacy and Applied Life Sciences）做研究工作一年。现为药理学教授，博士生导师，临床药学系主任。兼职神经药理专业委员会委员，山东药理学会常务理事，《山东药理学通讯》主编。发表学术论文百余篇，10余篇英文原著被SCI收载或引用。获山东省科学技术进步奖、省高校优秀8项。主编的著作和教材有《神经药理学研究技术与方法》（，2005年3月），《基础药理学》（高等教育出版社，2006年6月），《书写世界现代医学史的巨人们-历届诺贝尔生理学或医学奖获得者的传奇业绩和人生》（，2006年6月），《分子药理学》（高等教育出版社，2007年5月），《神经药理学》（人民卫生出版社，2008年2月）。另外，主译著作1部，副主编专著1部，参编著作1部，参编教材或教学参考书8本。主要从事细胞内Ca2+调控与神经系统疾病的关系探讨，以及ULMWH抗脑缺血、抗AD作用及其机制的系列研究。近年来发表的论文和出版的著作主要有：1. R. China.PROFILEQingzhu Zhang is the Male, the Han Nationality, born in Jinning city of Shandong province on Sept.24, 1956. B.S., graduated from major in medicine.of Qingdao Medical College in 1983. In 1999, he obtained his Ph.D. of Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences & Peking Union Medical College (CAMS & PUMC). Now, he is Prof. in Pharmacology, the tutor of Ph.D. student, and the director of Department of Clinical Pharmacy, Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Shandong University. He also is the member in Neuropharmacological Committee of the Chinese Pharmacological Society, and the member of standing council of Shandong Pharmacological Society. His research direction is Neuropharmacology, including the effects and mechanisms of melatonin, antioxidants and calcium antagonists on brain disorders or the aged diseases, especially in the relations between antagonists of inositol trisphosphate (IP3) receptor and Ca2+-modulated intracellular Ca2+ releases, and the treatment of Alzheimer’s disease. He holds the second, and third awards of Science and Technology Progressive Prizes (Government and Educational Committee of Shandong Province). 10 academic books were published, two of them in chief-in-editor.
