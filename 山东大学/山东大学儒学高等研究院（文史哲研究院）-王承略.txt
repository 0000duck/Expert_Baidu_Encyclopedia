1967年1月生，山东诸城人，文学博士。本科毕业于武汉大学图书馆学系。教授、博士生导师。中国诗经学会理事。研究领域为中国古典文献学、中国学术史。研究方向为先秦两汉文史文献、中国经学史、目录版本校勘学。代表性著作有《郑玄与今古文经学》、《李焘学行诗文辑考》。代表性论文有《刘向校书同僚学行考论》、《墨家学说体系的重新认识和思考》、《论毛诗序的写作年代》。现正主持编纂整理《二十五史艺文经籍志考补萃编》、《中华儒藏·诗经类》等。为硕士生开设《中国古典目录学史》、《汉书艺文志研究》、《诗经文献研究》、《二十世纪学术史文献》等课程。为博士生开设《目录学与中国学术思想史》。Biography:Wang Chenglue, was born in Zhucheng, Shandong Province, P. R. China, is teaching in the Institute of Literature, History and Philosophy in Shandong University as a full professor and supervisor for PhD students. As one of the directors in China’s Academy of the Book of Songs, he specializes in the study of Chinese Classical Philology as well as the History of Chinese Confucian Classics. And his research interests include: the Literary and History Ancient Documents in the periods before Qin Dynasty and in Han Dynasty, Chinese Classical History, the Bibliographical History Study of Edition Textual Criticism. He has authored two books, which were named Zheng Xuan and the New Text Scholarship, the Compilation and Study on Li Tao’s Academic Achievements of Poems or Articles and Life Experience. Meanwhile, he has being in charge of editing and coordinating the Assembly and Amendment of the Bibliographies of Science and Technology in Book Catalogues of the Twenty-five Histories, the Books of Shijing in Chinese Confucian Classics. He has published a great amount of papers such as the Discussion and Study on Liu Xiang’s Academic Achievements of Poems and Articles and Life Experience, the Reconsideration in Mohism, the Discussion on the Writing Year of Mao's Poetry. He teaches the Chinese Ancient Bibliographical History, the Bibliographies of Science and Technology recorded in Han Shu., the Documentary Study on the Books of Songs and the study on the academic
